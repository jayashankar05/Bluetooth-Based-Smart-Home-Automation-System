## What the Demo Video Should Show

1. **Hardware Setup Overview** – All components laid out and connected.
2. **Power ON** – HC-05 LED blinking (searching mode).
3. **Bluetooth Pairing** – Pairing smartphone with HC-05.
4. **App Connection** – Connecting Serial Bluetooth Terminal to HC-05.
5. **Individual Appliance Control** – Sending commands 1–8, showing each relay click and appliance ON/OFF.
6. **ALL ON / ALL OFF** – Sending 'A' and 'Z' commands.
7. **Serial Monitor Output** – Showing debug messages in Arduino IDE.
8. **Range Test** – Walking away from the circuit and testing commands at distance.

## Recording Tips

- Use OBS Studio or your phone's screen recorder to capture both the app screen and the hardware simultaneously.
- Good lighting helps show the relay LED indicators clearly.
- Keep video duration between 3–5 minutes for GitHub portfolio purposes.
- Add text overlays for each command being sent.
